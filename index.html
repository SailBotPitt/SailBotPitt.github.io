Sailbot

A Fully Autonomous Sailboat
Designed, Programmed and Fabricated by Pitt Students

Sailbot is a project based club that aims to compete in the annual International Robotic Sailing Competition. The sailboat will be tested in numerous aspects of its design including autonomous navigation, speed optimization, pathfinding and endurance. Over the course of a year we are tasked with designing, programming and fabricating this autonomous sailbot all while gaining experience from other students and learning new skills outside of the classroom.
